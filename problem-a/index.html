Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the protagonist of the hit Shonen Jump series Naruto and Naruto Shippuden. He is a ninja of the Uzumaki clan and the jinchuriki of the Nine-Tails.

  • He's also known as "The Show-off, Number One Unpredictacble, Noisy Ninja" - what's not to like about that!
  • Naruto's kind of the underdog, despite being the protagonist. He sets his dreams high and doesn't ever give up, even when the odds are stacked against him.
  • He's also really slow (not very bright), but he makes up for it with hard work and determination.
